## Snacking Tips for Your Conditions

### For Blood Sugar Balance
- Always pair carbs with protein or fat
- Choose snacks with fiber
- Avoid snacking on refined carbs alone
- Keep portion sizes moderate

### For Heart Health
- Choose unsalted nuts when possible
- Include fiber-rich snacks daily
- Limit cheese portions
- Opt for plant-based options often

### For Thyroid Support
- Include Brazil nuts (1-2/day max for selenium)
- Choose anti-inflammatory options
- Limit soy-based snacks near medication time
- Stay well-hydrated

### For Busy Mom Life
- Prep snacks on Sunday for the week
- Keep grab-and-go options ready
- Stock the diaper bag with nuts and fruit
- Share snack time with the kids
