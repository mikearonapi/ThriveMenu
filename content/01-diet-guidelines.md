## Key Nutritional Priorities

### 1. Anti-Inflammatory Foods (Graves' Disease)
- Wild-caught fatty fish (salmon, sardines, mackerel)
- Colorful vegetables and berries
- Olive oil and avocados
- Nuts and seeds (especially Brazil nuts for selenium)
- Turmeric, ginger, and anti-inflammatory spices

### 2. Heart-Healthy Choices (Cholesterol Management)
- Soluble fiber (oats, beans, lentils, barley)
- Plant sterols (nuts, seeds, legumes)
- Omega-3 fatty acids (fatty fish, walnuts, flaxseed)
- Limit saturated fats
- Avoid trans fats completely

### 3. Blood Sugar Balance (Diabetes Prevention)
- Low glycemic index carbohydrates
- Pair carbs with protein and healthy fats
- High fiber foods
- Consistent meal timing
- Portion awareness with starches

### 4. Thyroid-Supportive Nutrients
- **Selenium**: Brazil nuts (1-2/day), fish, eggs
- **Zinc**: Pumpkin seeds, chickpeas, lean meats
- **Vitamin D**: Fatty fish, egg yolks, fortified foods
- **Iron**: Leafy greens, legumes, lean meats

## Timing & Lifestyle Tips

### Meal Timing for Thyroid Medication
- Take thyroid medication on an empty stomach (30-60 min before eating)
- Avoid calcium-rich foods and soy near medication time
- Coffee should wait until after medication absorption

### Blood Sugar Balance
- Eat regular meals (don't skip)
- Include protein at every meal
- Pair carbs with fat and protein
- Consider smaller, more frequent meals

### For Busy Mom Life
- Batch cook on weekends
- Keep healthy snacks accessible
- Prep ingredients in advance
- Simple doesn't mean unhealthy
- Involve kids in cooking when possible
